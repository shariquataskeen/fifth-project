Spotlight Exhibitions

Spotlight Exhibitions Spotlight is an ongoing-curated exhibition series, in exploration of the digital art landscape. Digital Art Fair supports and showcases artists at the forefront of the intersection of art and technology, in an attempt to broaden the understandings and scope of digital art into realms that are familiar and new at the same time.

Spotlight Exhibitions

DIGITAL ART FAIR 2023

In our most ambitious Art Fair yet, we connected global galleries to our regional partners at K11 MUSEA, The Silicone Valley of Art and Culture in Hong Kong. Welcoming 10,000+ guests and achieving over $6 million in sales, furthering Digital Art Fair's positioning in the digital art and Web 3.0 space.

DIGITAL ART FAIR 2022

The second edition of Digital Art Fair welcomed more than 10,000 visitors over the 18 days from 20 October – 6 November 2022. In collaboration with Sotheby’s, the fair’s auctioneer partner, Digital Art Fair’s Xperience Digital Art Auction achieved over $8 million HKD in sales revenue. Digital Art Auction.

DIGITAL ART FAIR 2021

With overwhelming response from the public, the inaugural Digital Art Fair Asia officially came to an end on 22 October 2021. The fair has welcomed more than 25,700 visitors and sold more than 400 Digital and Physical Art for a combined HK$54.5 million (US$7 million) during a 23-day period.
