ABOUT DIGITAL ART FAIR

Digital Art Fair is the leading Web 3.0 annual art fair in the world of the digital art era. Showcasing some of the most innovative and exciting digital art and NFTs from around the globe. The fair provides a platform for emerging and established artists to exhibit their work, and for art enthusiasts and collectors to discover and acquire new digital artwork. With the vision to cultivate and inspire a new generation of art collectors to embrace the use of digital technology to explore the beauty of art and culture.
